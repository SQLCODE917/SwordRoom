## 0) Dice notation and rounding

### Dice
- **1D**: roll 1 six-sided die (result 1ŌĆō6).
- **2D**: roll 2 six-sided dice and add them (result 2ŌĆō12).
- **1/2D**: take **half the result of 1D**, **rounded up**.

If a rule says to divide and does **not** say how to round, **round up**.

- Dwarf
- Grassrunner
- Elf

### 3.1 Dwarf (rule characteristics)
- Can see in the dark.
- Has **level 5** in **one** craftsman general skill: **carpentry, smithing, woodworking, stonemasonry, or gem crafting**.
- Unable to learn **ancient magic** or **spirit magic**. ŅłĆfileciteŅłéturn6file1Ņłü

- Can communicate with **plants and insects**.

### Step 4 ŌĆö Decide age, gender, name (1.4.4)

These are **not numeric** in the core rules: no ability differences by gender or age in normal creation. ŅłĆfileciteŅłéturn6file1Ņłü

#### Age rules (core)
- You are **not** fully free to pick any age.
- For a **human**, a good range is **15 to around 30**. ŅłĆfileciteŅłéturn6file1Ņłü
- Human lifespan is about **100**, but the average in Alecrast is about **50** due to poor medical care. ŅłĆfileciteŅłéturn6file1Ņłü

- Grassrunners: around **40**
- Elves: around **100**
- Half-elves:

#### Gender (core)
- No mechanical differences.
- All races have male/female as with humans. ŅłĆfileciteŅłéturn6file1Ņłü

#### Names
- Use a name appropriate to Alecrast; usually western-style.

## 6) Restrictions on weapons/armor based on skills (1.4.7)

General idea:
- If you want to fully use a skill, you must obey its equipment limits.
- If a character has both restricted and unrestricted skills, equipment must match the **restricted** skill to fully use both. ŅłĆfileciteŅłéturn6file1Ņłü

### Fighter
- No restrictions.
- Match required strength to your Strength. ŅłĆfileciteŅłéturn6file1Ņłü

### Thief
To fully use thief skill:
- Weapons/armor must be light: required strength up to **half your Strength (rounded up)**.
- Armor: **metal armor other than ring mail may not be used**, regardless of strength/required strength.
- Choose **non-metal armor** or **ring mail**. ŅłĆfileciteŅłéturn6file1Ņłü

### Ranger
To fully use ranger skill:
- Armor must be light: required strength up to **half your Strength (rounded up)**.
- Armor: **metal armor other than ring mail may not be used**, regardless of strength/required strength.
- Choose **non-metal armor** or **ring mail**.
- Weapons: no restrictions, but rangerŌĆÖs combat edge is **projectiles** (throwable / made for throwing / shooting). ŅłĆfileciteŅłéturn6file1Ņłü

### Sorcerer
To fully use sorcerer skill:
- Armor must be **cloth** or **soft leather** (choose the strongest within your allowed range).
- Do **not** buy a shield.
- Weapon: must buy a **mageŌĆÖs staff** (essential for ancient magic).
  - If Strength Ōēż 10: buy a staff with required strength equal to your Strength.
  - If Strength Ōēź 10: buy a staff with required strength **10**. ŅłĆfileciteŅłéturn6file1Ņłü

### Shaman
To fully use shaman skill:
- Armor limited to **cloth**, **soft leather**, or **hard leather** (choose the strongest within your allowed range).
- Do **not** buy a shield.
- Weapon: since you canŌĆÖt use a shield, prefer a **two-handed** weapon if possible; match required strength to Strength. ŅłĆfileciteŅłéturn6file1Ņłü

### Sage / Bard / Priest
- No inherent equipment restrictions.
- If you also have restricted skills, follow those restrictions. ŅłĆfileciteŅłéturn6file1Ņłü

- Humans: treat **prime age (around 15ŌĆō50)** as **modifier 1**.
- Use **Table 18-1** for other ages.
- For non-human races: treat them as equal to humans **until adulthood**.
- After adulthood (and after the age where ability scores start decreasing), apply special race handling:
  - **Elves:** aging is not considered.
  - **Dwarves / Grassrunners / Half-Elves:** aging begins at **100**; **divide age by half** and apply to the human table. ŅłĆfileciteŅłéturn6file0Ņłü
